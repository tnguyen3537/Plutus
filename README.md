# Plutus

An economic analytics platform that tracks weekly macroeconomic indicators and major market indices with deep analytics, presented on an interactive web dashboard.

## What it does

- **Descriptive analytics** — visualize interest rates, inflation, unemployment, and stock indices over time across international markets
- **Predictive modeling** — weekly forecasts on key macroeconomic series
- **Market sentiment** — sentiment scored from financial news and social text, aligned to the tracked series
